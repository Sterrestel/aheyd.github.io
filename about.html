ABOUT

Hi, I go by Abi or Locke!

I'm a graduate student working on my masters in library science. I'm 24 and I've been writing for nearly ten years. When I’m not writing, I draw, play video games, read, cross stitch, bullet journal, and dabble in photography during my free time. Some of my favorite games are Minecraft, Stardew Valley, Coral Island, Guild Wars 2, Spiritfarer, and The Evil Within.

My work probably fits quite loosely under fantasy, but I often dip into all sorts of genres, including but not limited to poetry, steampunk, dystopian, science fiction, supernatural, and more.
